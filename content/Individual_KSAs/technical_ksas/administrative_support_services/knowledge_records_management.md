proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Locates and files records using established naming conventions; recognizes basic retention schedules. 
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Maintains departmental records library; applies retention or privacy tags in an ECM system; flags mis‑filed items. 
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Designs folder taxonomies and metadata schemas; audits compliance with HIPAA, GDPR, or FOIA retention rules.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Leads org‑wide records clean‑up and migration to a cloud ECM; integrates search, OCR, and e‑discovery tooling. 
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Architects enterprise knowledge‑governance strategy aligning legal, risk, and data‑value objectives; mentors RM professionals.
