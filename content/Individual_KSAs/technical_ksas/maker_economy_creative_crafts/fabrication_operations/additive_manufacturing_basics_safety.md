proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies printer types; recognizes filament/resin hazards; follows basic PPE usage.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Slices models with correct layer height; loads material; monitors first-layer adhesion; performs post-processing safely.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Troubleshoots print defects; calibrates extrusion parameters; manages resin curing and disposal per SDS.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements enclosure ventilation; profiles new materials; conducts preventive maintenance schedules.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs makerspace AM safety policies; trains community members; publishes open-source print-quality benchmarks.
