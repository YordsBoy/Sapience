    indicator: Develops Incident Action Plans (IAPs); chairs briefings; deploys mutual aid.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Manages multi‑operational periods; coordinates Unified Command; conducts after‑action reviews.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads regional IMTs (Incident Management Teams); shapes NIMS policy updates; publishes ICS best practices.
