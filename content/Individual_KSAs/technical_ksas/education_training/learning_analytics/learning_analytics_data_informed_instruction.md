  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Utilises predictive‑risk models; correlates engagement with achievement; applies anonymization protocols.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs randomised‑controlled trials; integrates multi‑source datasets; measures intervention efficacy.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads institution‑wide learning‑analytics programs; publishes open datasets; advises on ethical AI in education.
