category: Technical
sector: Public_Safety_Security
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "CUAS"
  - "Airspace Security"
description: Detecting, tracking, and mitigating rogue unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) at sensitive sites using RF, radar, optical sensors, and kinetic/non‑kinetic mitigation in line with FAA and DHS guidelines.
source_frameworks:
  - "FAA Remote-ID Rule"
  - "FAA UAS Facility Maps"
  - "DoD CUAS Strategy"
  - "DHS CISA Counter‑UAS Guidance"
  - "ASTM F3325‑20 UAS Detection Standard"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies UAS threats; recognises detection technologies; observes legal constraints.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Deploys RF scanners; maps geo‑fencing zones; logs drone sightings; classifies drone; escalates alert.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Integrates multi‑sensor fusion; analyses flight telemetry; performs risk-based response; coordinates law‑enforcement response.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements mitigation (jamming, net guns) where authorised; conducts red‑team testing; logs evidence; tests SOP; briefs stakeholders.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Develops counter‑UAS policy; leads national programmes; publishes threat assessments.
