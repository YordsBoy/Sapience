  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Integrates demand‑side platforms (DSP); optimises ROAS (Return on Ad Spend); applies attribution models.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Launches offsite campaigns; negotiates brand partnerships; aligns reporting to IAB standards.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads enterprise RMN strategy; shapes industry measurement standards; publishes RMN monetisation research.
