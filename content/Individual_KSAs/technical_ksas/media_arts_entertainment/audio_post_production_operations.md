label: Professional Audio Post‑production Operations
category: Technical
sector: Media_Arts_Entertainment
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags: ["Audio Post","Mixing","Mastering"]
description: Editing, mixing, and mastering linear and immersive audio for film, television, and streaming using industry standards—Audio Engineering Society (AES) loudness specs, Dolby Atmos workflows—and ensuring deliverables meet distribution requirements.
source_frameworks: ["AES TD1008 Loudness Guidelines","Dolby Atmos Home Entertainment Mix Spec"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies digital‑audio‑workstation (DAW) tools; recognises loudness units full scale (LUFS) meters; imports OMF/AAF sessions.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Performs dialogue editing; applies basic equalisation (EQ) and dynamics; conforms stems to cue sheet.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Mixes 5.1 surround; calibrates monitoring environments; meets Integrated Loudness targets per EBU R128.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Builds object‑based Atmos mixes; implements noise‑reduction; conducts deliverable QC for streaming platforms.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Supervises multi‑studio post workflows; sets facility standards; mentors re‑recording mixers; publishes audio‑post best practices.
