proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Recognises trauma signs; uses calming voice; maintains safe distance.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Applies PFA steps; employs de‑escalation techniques; refers to services.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts risk assessments; collaborates with mental‑health professionals; documents interventions.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Trains peers in trauma‑informed care; leads CIT programmes; tracks outcome metrics.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Shapes trauma‑policy; coordinates multi‑agency crisis hubs; publishes intervention studies.
