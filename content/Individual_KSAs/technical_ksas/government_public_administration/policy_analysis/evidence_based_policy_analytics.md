description: Leveraging administrative data, statistical methods, and program‑evaluation techniques to formulate, implement, and review public policies in compliance with the Foundations for Evidence‑Based Policymaking Act and OMB Circular A‑11.
source_frameworks:
  - "OECD Recommendation on Public-Policy Evaluation"

  - "Evidence Act of 2018"

  - "GAO Program Evaluation Guide"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies data sources; understands logic‑model basics; distinguishes outputs vs. outcomes.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Collects administrative datasets; performs descriptive statistics; drafts evaluation questions.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts quasi‑experimental designs; applies machine‑learning prediction to policy targeting; visualises findings for stakeholders.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates cross‑agency data; manages privacy‑preserving record linkage; publishes Evidence Act Learning Agendas.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads national policy‑evaluation portfolios; standardises evidence‑quality criteria; advises Congress on data‑driven legislation.
