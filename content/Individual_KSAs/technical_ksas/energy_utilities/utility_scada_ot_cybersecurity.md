  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies key SCADA components—Remote Terminal Units (RTUs), Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs); recognises basic cyber‑hygiene practices.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Applies network‑segmentation principles; updates firmware; implements role‑based access controls.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts vulnerability assessments; deploys anomaly‑detection sensors; responds to incident alerts.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs Defence‑in‑Depth architectures; aligns policies to NERC CIP; leads incident‑response tabletop exercises.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Oversees utility‑wide OT‑security programmes; collaborates with national cyber‑defence centres; influences standards development.
