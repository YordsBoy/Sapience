proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies single-point failures; tracks lead times.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Maps suppliers; keeps safety stock; uses alternative transport options.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Pools orders with peers; digitises orders via messaging apps; monitors risk indicators.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Negotiates contingency contracts; accesses emergency micro-loans; shares storage.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Advises community clusters; lobbies for inclusive relief policies; mentors entrepreneurs.
