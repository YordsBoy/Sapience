category: Technical
sector: Nonprofit_NGOs

cluster_tags:
  - "Safeguarding"
  - "Risk"
description: >
  Implements safeguarding policy and risk registers in line with the Sphere Handbook Protection Principles and ISO 31000 risk guidelines, preventing exploitation, abuse, fraud, and corruption.
source_frameworks:
  - "Sphere Handbook Protection Principles"
  - "ISO 31000:2018 Risk Management"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines safeguarding vs child-protection; lists incident types.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Completes background checks; logs incidents; escalates per policy.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts risk assessment; updates register; trains volunteers.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Audits third-party partners; aligns insurance; reports to board.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Chairs safeguarding committee; liaises with donors; mentors sector peers.
