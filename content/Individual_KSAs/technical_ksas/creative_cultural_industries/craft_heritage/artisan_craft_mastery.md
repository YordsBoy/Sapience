proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies regional craft styles; uses basic hand tools safely; follows cultural protocols.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Produces simple craft items; selects eco‑friendly materials; documents process with photography.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Combines traditional methods with Computer‑Aided Design (CAD); operates small‑batch kilns or looms; prices items for local markets.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Mentors apprentices; integrates digital fabrication (CNC, laser cutting); curates exhibitions; manages online storefronts.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Revives endangered crafts; consults museums; leads international residencies; publishes craft‑innovation research.
