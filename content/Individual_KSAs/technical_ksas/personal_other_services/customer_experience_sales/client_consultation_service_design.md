proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Greets client; lists open‑ended questions; records basic preferences; summarises agreed services.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Utilises consultation forms; performs patch test; explains options & service outcomes; confirms price sets; gains informed consent for treatments.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Uses digital consultation form; captures before/after photos; aligns expectations; applies empathy‑mapping; prototypes service options; uses visual aids for decision‑making.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Tailors service bundles; tracks consultation conversion rates; integrates accessibility considerations.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Coaches staff on design‑thinking consultations; embeds co‑creation into brand standards; publishes consultation script; raises re-book rate; publishes consultation‑outcome studies.
