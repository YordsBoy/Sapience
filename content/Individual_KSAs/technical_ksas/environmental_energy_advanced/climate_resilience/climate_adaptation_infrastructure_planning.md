cluster_tags:
  - "Climate Adaptation"
  - "Infrastructure"
description: >
  Integrates climate-hazard projections into infrastructure siting, design, and O&M; applies nature-based solutions, resilience metrics, and financing models.
source_frameworks:
  - "UNEP Adaptation Gap Report (United Nations Environment Programme — intergovernmental organization; publicly available)"
  - "UNEP Nature-based Solutions for Climate Adaptation guidelines (United Nations Environment Programme — intergovernmental organization; publicly available)"
  - "ASCE 7-22 Minimum Design Loads and Associated Criteria for Buildings and Other Structures (proprietary standard — American Society of Civil Engineers; conceptual alignment only)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies climate hazards; reviews design codes; estimates exposure.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Calculates freeboard elevations; sizes detention basins; selects resilient materials; screens projects with climate-risk checklists.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Uses downscaled data for design loads; conducts cost-benefit adaptation analysis; integrates NbS; coordinates stakeholder workshops.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Develops regional adaptation plans; secures funding; implements adaptive O&M regimes; monitors resilience KPIs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads national or portfolio-level adaptation strategies; shapes climate-resilient codes; publishes adaptation research.
