label: Value-Based Care Data & Quality Metrics
category: Technical
sector: Healthcare_Social_Assistance
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "Value-Based Care"
  - "Quality Metrics"
description: >
  Collecting, analysing, and reporting clinical and financial metrics—quality measures, patient‑reported outcomes, cost‑of‑care indicators—to support value‑based payment models, risk adjustment, and continuous improvement.
source_frameworks:
  - "Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) Merit‑based Incentive Payment System (MIPS) Quality Measures"
  - "National Quality Forum (NQF) Measure Evaluation Framework"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Lists common quality measures (e.g., readmission rates); recognises basic cost‑of‑care terminology; references MIPS categories.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Extracts measure data from electronic health records (EHRs); calculates numerator and denominator; validates data sources; submits data via Quality Payment Program (QPP) portals.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Performs risk adjustment and benchmarking; links clinical outcomes to claims data; visualises composite scores; submits QRDA/IQR files.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs dashboards for accountable‑care organizations (ACOs); models shared‑savings forecasts; integrates social‑risk stratification; leads gap-closure initiatives; integrates feedback loops.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Shapes organisation’s VBC contract strategy; leads value‑based contracting analytics; advises on national measure‑development; publishes research on value‑based performance; collaborates with CMS/NCQA.
