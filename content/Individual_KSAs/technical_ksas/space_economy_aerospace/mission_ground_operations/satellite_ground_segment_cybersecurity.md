  - "Ground Segment"

description: >
  Protecting ground-segment networks, TT&C links, and payload data against cyber threats, applying NIST SP 800-160, Space ISAC, and Zero-Trust best practices.
source_frameworks:
  - "NIST IR 8401 Satellite Ground System Security Considerations (2022) — U.S. government — NIST/DOC; publicly available"
  - "NIST SP 800-160 Vol. 2 Developing Cyber-Resilient Systems — U.S. government — NIST/DOC; publicly available"
  - "Space Information Sharing and Analysis Center (Space ISAC) cybersecurity best practices — non-profit sector consortium — Space ISAC; conceptual alignment only"
  - "CISA space and launch system cybersecurity guidance — U.S. government — CISA/DHS; publicly available"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Recognises threat vectors; uses basic encryption; monitors login attempts; enforces MFA for operators.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Configures firewalls; applies secure-telemetry protocols (CP-Sec); manages access controls.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts vulnerability scans; implements intrusion detection; manages key-distribution for uplink; maps controls to NIST CSF.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs zero-trust architectures; deploys zero-trust micro-segmentation; integrates threat-intel feeds; performs red and blue-team exercises.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads space-asset cybersecurity programs; establishes enterprise ground-segment SOC; coordinates incident response with national CERTs; publishes cyber-resilience frameworks.
