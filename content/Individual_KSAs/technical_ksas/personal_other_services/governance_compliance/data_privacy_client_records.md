  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Explains PII vs PHI; secures paper files.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Uses password manager; obtains consent; shreddes documents.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Encrypts backups; sets role-based access; logs breaches.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Conducts DPIA; contracts DPA clauses; trains staff annually.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Achieves ISO 27001; mentors peers; advises associations.
