description: Implementing safety and well-being measures for on-demand workers—rideshare drivers, delivery couriers, remote freelancers—covering personal security, ergonomic practices, and incident-reporting mechanisms within digital platforms.
source_frameworks:
  - "TransUnion gig worker safety and risk research (2025) — commercial consumer data firm; conceptual alignment only"

proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Recognises common platform-work hazards (robbery, fraud, deactivation); uses in-app emergency features; follows basic hygiene protocols.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Conducts pre-shift vehicle/equipment checks; applies ergonomic workstation setups; confirms drop-off PIN codes; enables two-factor authentication; logs incidents in platform portal.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements route-risk assessments; utilizes personal-safety devices; reports unsafe clients; appeals unjust deactivations; engages in peer-safety forums.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs safety-training modules; installs dashcams; analyses incident data for patterns; educates peers; collaborates with platform on hazard mitigation.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads industry safety-standard initiatives; advises regulators; publishes research on gig-economy occupational safety.
