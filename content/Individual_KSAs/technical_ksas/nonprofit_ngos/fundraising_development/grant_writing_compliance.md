ksa_id: grant_writing_compliance
label: Grant‑Writing & Compliance
category: Technical
sector: Nonprofit_NGO
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Grant Compliance"
  - "Reporting"
description: Researching funding opportunities, crafting compelling grant proposals, and ensuring post‑award financial and programmatic compliance with donor regulations such as Uniform Guidance (2 CFR 200) and EU PRAG.
source_frameworks:
  - "U.S. Uniform Guidance 2 CFR 200"
  - "USAID ADS 303"
  - "Grants.gov Community Blog"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Searches grant databases; defines allowable vs allocable cost; recognises restricted items; summarises eligibility criteria; compiles required attachments.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Aligns project goals to donor priorities; drafts narratives; prepares line‑item budgets; maps budget to SF-424; logs sub-awards; schedules reports.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Coordinates multi‑partner proposals; manages compliance checklists; tracks timekeeping; maintains procurement files; responds to donor audits.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Negotiates indirect‑cost rates; implements grant management systems; oversees financial reporting; calculates NICRA fringe; closes grants; recovers indirect cost.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Directs compliance unit;; leads grant‑acquisition strategy; audits compliance across portfolio; trains grant professionals.
