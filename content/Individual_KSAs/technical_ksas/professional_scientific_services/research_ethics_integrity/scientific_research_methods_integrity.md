---
ksa_id: scientific_research_methods_integrity
label: Scientific Research Methods & Integrity
category: Technical
sector: professional_scientific_services
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Research Methods"
  - "Integrity"
description: Designing and conducting rigorous scientific studies while upholding research-integrity principles—authorship, data transparency, avoidance of fabrication—per OECD Frascati Manual and Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) guidelines.
source_frameworks:
  - "CONSORT 2010 Statement — Consolidated Standards of Reporting Trials (international reporting standard consortium; publicly available)"
  - "FAIR Data Principles — Findability, Accessibility, Interoperability, Reusability (GO FAIR international initiative; open-access community standard; publicly available)"
  - "OECD Frascati Manual 2015 — Proposed Standard Practice for Surveys on Research and Experimental Development (intergovernmental publication — OECD; publicly available)"
  - "COPE Core Practices (Committee on Publication Ethics; international nonprofit membership organization; publicly available)"
  - "National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine report on Fostering Integrity in Research 2017 (U.S. national academies publication; publicly available)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Formulates testable hypotheses; identifies bias types; distinguishes qualitative vs. quantitative methods; cites sources properly.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Designs sampling strategies; selects appropriate design; preregisters protocol; manages data-collection protocols; registers studies on open registries.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Applies statistical-power analysis; applies randomisation; maintains version-controlled data; conducts peer debriefs.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements preregistration; performs reproducibility checks; passes peer-review integrity checks; shares FAIR dataset; addresses conflicts of interest.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Chairs institutional review boards (IRBs); leads meta-research initiatives; shapes national integrity policies.
