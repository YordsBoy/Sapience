label: Document Preparation & Editing
category: Technical
sector: Administrative_Support
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Document Production"
  - "Editing"
description: >
  Drafts, formats, and edits business documents—memos, reports, presentations—
  ensuring clarity, consistency, and brand style; uses advanced word-processing
  and desktop-publishing tools.
source_frameworks:
  - "Microsoft 365 Style Guide"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Applies basic formatting (fonts, spacing).
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Uses templates; checks spelling/grammar; saves PDFs.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Applies styles, section breaks; edits for tone; inserts cross-references.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Creates complex layouts; merges data (mail merge); enforces accessibility rules.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Manages enterprise templates; trains teams; integrates docs with translation/localisation workflows.
