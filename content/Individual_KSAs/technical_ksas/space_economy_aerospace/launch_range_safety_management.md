  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts Monte Carlo trajectory analyses; prepares launch readiness reviews (LRRs); coordinates range assets.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs autonomous flight‑safety systems; negotiates risk‑criterion waivers; manages anomaly investigations.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Oversees multi‑range safety programs; influences regulatory updates; publishes launch‑safety research.
