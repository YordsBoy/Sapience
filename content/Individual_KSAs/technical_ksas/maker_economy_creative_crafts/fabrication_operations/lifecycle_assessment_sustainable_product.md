label: Life-Cycle Assessment for Sustainable Products
category: Technical
sector: Maker_Creative_Crafts
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "LCA"
  - "Sustainability"
description: >
  Conducts cradle-to-grave life-cycle assessment (LCA) using ISO 14040 methodology and open-source tools (OpenLCA, Eco-Invent datasets) to quantify carbon and resource footprints.
source_frameworks:
  - "ISO 14040 Life-Cycle Assessment"
  - "OpenLCA Practitioner Guide"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines functional unit, system boundary, impact category.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Collects material weights; enters process flows; reviews impact chart.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Compares alternative materials; identifies hot spots; suggests reductions.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Publishes simplified Product Environmental Footprint (PEF); integrates LCA into design loop.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads circular-design program; mentors makers; contributes data to databases.
