sector: Administrative_Support
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "Procurement"

description: >
  Sources office supplies and services, manages stock, processes purchase
  orders, and reconciles invoices while applying budgeting controls and vendor
  KPIs.
source_frameworks:
  - "ARMA Inventory Best Practice"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Monitors low-stock items; follows reorder lists.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Creates POs; verifies deliveries; matches invoices.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Negotiates quotes; maintains inventory system; prevents stock-outs.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Conducts spend analysis; applies supplier KPIs; audits sustainability compliance.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads strategic sourcing; mentors staff; integrates procurement analytics dashboards.
