proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Explains double-coincidence of wants; lists local barter platforms.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Prices goods in barter credits; records transactions; resolves simple disputes.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Calculates tax equivalence where required; promotes marketplace trust; tracks inventory rotation.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs community barter events; mitigates default risk; maintains ledger transparency using mobile apps.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Advises municipalities on complementary-currency policy; mentors exchange coordinators.
