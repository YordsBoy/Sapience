label: Scientific Research Methods & Integrity
category: Technical
sector: Professional_Scientific_Services
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags: ["Research Methods","Integrity","Reproducibility"]
description: Designing and conducting rigorous scientific studies while upholding research‑integrity principles—authorship, data transparency, avoidance of fabrication—per OECD *Frascati Manual* and Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) guidelines.
source_frameworks: ["OECD Frascati Manual 2015","COPE Core Practices","National Academies Research Integrity Report 2023"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Formulates testable hypotheses; distinguishes qualitative vs. quantitative methods; cites sources properly.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Designs sampling strategies; manages data‑collection protocols; registers studies on open registries.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Applies statistical‑power analysis; maintains version‑controlled data; conducts peer debriefs.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements preregistration; performs reproducibility checks; addresses conflicts of interest.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Chairs institutional review boards (IRBs); leads meta‑research initiatives; shapes national integrity policies.
