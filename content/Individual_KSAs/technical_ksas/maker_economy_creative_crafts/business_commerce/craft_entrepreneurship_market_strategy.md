  - level: Basic
    indicator: Calculates cost of goods sold (COGS); sets retail price; opens online shop; photographs products for online listings; writes search‑optimised descriptions; manages small‑scale inventory.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Utilises social‑media marketing; analyses click-through and conversion metrics; iterates product photos; participates in craft fairs.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Diversifies sales channels; registers trademarks; implements sustainable packaging; negotiates wholesale contracts; implements retargeting ads; meets 30 % repeat-purchase rate..
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Launches brand collaborations; scales craft business internationally; mentors artisans on digital commerce; publishes craft‑enterprise case studies.
