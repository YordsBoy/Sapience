ksa_id: public_private_partnership_management
label: Public-Private Partnership Management
category: Technical
sector: Government_Public_Admin
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "PPP"
  - "Infrastructure"
description: >
  Structures, negotiates, and manages public-private partnerships (PPP) using World Bank PPP Reference Guide and International Project Finance Association (IPFA) best practices to deliver infrastructure and services.
source_frameworks:
  - "World Bank PPP Reference Guide"
  - "IPFA Knowledge Hub"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines PPP models (BOT, DBFO) and risk-allocation principles.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Screens projects; drafts pre-feasibility study; sets Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Runs value-for-money (VfM) analysis; manages tender; negotiates term sheet.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Oversees financial close; monitors contract compliance; renegotiates amendments.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads national PPP unit; structures multi-billion portfolio; mentors sector agencies.
