proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Recognises burnout signs; lists respite types; gathers contact info.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Schedules respite sessions; completes intake forms; communicates care instructions.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Evaluates provider credentials; secures funding assistance; maintains care logs.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Develops contingency plans; coordinates multi‑provider schedules; tracks relief outcomes.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads regional respite networks; secures grant funding; publishes caregiver‑relief research.
