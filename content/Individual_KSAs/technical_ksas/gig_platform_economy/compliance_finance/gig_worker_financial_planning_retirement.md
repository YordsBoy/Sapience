category: Technical

  - "Finance"
  - "Retirement"
description: >
  Builds emergency funds, selects self-employed retirement vehicles (Solo 401(k), SEP-IRA), and manages variable cash flow to ensure long-term financial security.
source_frameworks:
  - "U.S. Department of Labor Self-Employed Retirement Resources"
  - "CFPB Financial Well-Being Toolkit"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Calculates irregular income cycles; identifies retirement-plan options.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Sets 3-month emergency fund; automates percentage-based transfers; tracks expenses.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Maximises tax-deductible contributions; purchases health insurance via marketplaces.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Diversifies income streams; uses low-cost index funds; models scenario projections.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Coaches gig community on financial wellness; advocates portable benefits; partners with fintech apps.
