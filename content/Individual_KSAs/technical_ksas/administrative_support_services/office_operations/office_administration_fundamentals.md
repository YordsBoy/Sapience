description: >
  Oversees day-to-day office functions—reception, supplies, mail, facilities
  requests—and coordinates with internal departments to maintain smooth
  operations and a professional environment.
source_frameworks:
  - "IAAP Certified Administrative Professional (CAP) Body of Knowledge (commercial professional association — International Association of Administrative Professionals; conceptual alignment only)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies the core services provided by an office administration function (mail handling, reception, supplies management, facilities coordination) and describes the role of each.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Logs facility tickets; reorders supplies; greets visitors.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Coordinates multiple vendors; updates office procedures; ensures workspace ergonomics.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Manages office budgets; implements sustainability initiatives; trains junior staff.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs office-services strategy; optimizes space utilization; leads cross-site coordination.
