---
ksa_id: trail_facility_maintenance
label: Trail & Facility Maintenance
category: Technical
sector: Arts_Recreation
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Outdoor"
  - "Maintenance"
description: >
  Inspects, repairs, and improves recreation trails and related facilities
  following US FS Trail Construction & Maintenance Notebook (2025 Edition) and
  FHWA Active-Transportation guidance.
source_frameworks:
  - "US Forest Service Trail Construction & Maintenance Notebook (2025)"
  - "FHWA Guide for Maintaining Active Transportation Infrastructure"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies trail hazards; performs litter removal.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Clears drainage; prunes vegetation; documents minor repairs.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Restores tread; installs signage; operates hand tools safely.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Leads volunteer crews; applies sustainable design (outslope, grade dips); estimates material needs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Develops maintenance plans; uses GIS for asset tracking; mentors stewards.
---
