category: Technical
sector: Government_Public_Administration

cluster_tags:
  - "Smart City"
  - "Cybersecurity"
description: >
  Oversees Internet of Things (IoT) deployments—traffic sensors, smart lighting—ensuring cybersecurity per International Organization for
  Standardization/International Electrotechnical Commission (ISO/IEC) 30141 architecture and National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
  800-213 IoT guidelines.
source_frameworks:
  - "ISO/IEC 30141 IoT Reference Architecture"
  - "NIST 800-213 IoT-Device Cybersecurity"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies smart-city subsystems; recognises basic threats (DDoS, spoofing).
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Documents asset inventory; enforces default-password change; applies patch policy.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Segments networks; implements Public Key Infrastructure (PKI); logs device telemetry.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Aligns controls with ISO/IEC 30141 domains; conducts penetration testing; manages incident response.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads citywide IoT-security strategy; integrates zero-trust architecture; mentors security engineers.
