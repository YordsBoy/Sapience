label: Sustainable Materials & Life‑Cycle Assessment (LCA)
category: Technical
sector: Environmental_Advanced_Energy
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "Sustainable Materials"
  - "Life Cycle"
description: >
  Evaluates material choices using embodied-carbon, toxicity, and circularity metrics; selects low-impact alternatives and supplier EPDs (environmental product declarations).
source_frameworks:
  - "EN 15804 Sustainable Construction"
  - "ISO 14040/44 LCA Standards"
  - "EN 15804+A2 Building EPD"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Reads EPDs; defines system boundaries; identifies impact categories.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Compares materials via EPD databases; uses LCA tools (SimaPro/OpenLCA); collects inventory data; calculates global‑warming potential (GWP).
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Performs sensitivity analysis; evaluates biogenic carbon; compares material options.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates LCA into BIM workflows; specifies bio-based or recycled materials at scale; publishes material passports; optimises circularity.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Develops material-circularity targets;; leads corporate LCA programs; shapes product‑category rules; publishes LCA methodology advancements.
