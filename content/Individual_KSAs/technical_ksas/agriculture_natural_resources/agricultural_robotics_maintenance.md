label: Agricultural Robotics Maintenance
category: Technical
sector: Agriculture_Natural_Resources
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags: ["AgTech","Robotics","Precision Farming"]
description: Installation, preventive maintenance, fault diagnosis, and repair of autonomous or semi‑autonomous robotic systems used in crop and livestock operations—for example, weeding robots, robotic harvesters, and swarms of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs).
source_frameworks: ["O*NET® 49‑9099.00 (Farm Equipment Mechanics)", "CREO 2025 Agricultural Robotics Report"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Performs basic sensor cleaning and battery swaps under supervision; follows lock‑out / tag‑out safety procedures.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Runs Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) diagnostics; replaces worn actuators or belts; calibrates simple Light Detection and Ranging (LiDAR) or camera modules.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Tunes Proportional‑Integral‑Derivative (PID) loops for navigation; updates Robot Operating System (ROS) packages; troubleshoots network or Controller Area Network (CAN) bus errors in the field.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates new tool‑heads; implements predictive‑maintenance dashboards using telemetry; optimises uptime across the fleet.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs maintenance programs for multi‑farm robotic fleets; leads root‑cause analysis and reliability‑engineering improvements.
