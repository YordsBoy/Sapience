description: >
  Structuring green bonds, sustainability-linked loans, and ESG disclosures, aligning with EU Taxonomy, Green Bond Principles, and Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) standards.
source_frameworks:
  - "ICMA Green Bond Principles (International Capital Market Association — commercial industry body; conceptual alignment only)"
  - "EU Taxonomy Climate Delegated Act (Regulation (EU) 2020/852 and delegated acts) (European Union regulation; publicly available)"
  - "GRI Standards 2024 (Global Reporting Initiative — nonprofit standards body; conceptual alignment only)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines green-finance instruments; lists ESG metrics; recognizes investor expectations.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Compiles ESG data; calculates KPI baselines; drafts TCFD-aligned climate disclosures; tracks KPIs.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Aligns projects to EU Taxonomy; engages second-party opinions; reports GHG reductions; manages bond frameworks.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Structures sustainability-linked loans; manages ongoing disclosure; assesses impact KPIs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads corporate green-finance strategy; advises regulators; publishes ESG-finance research.
