  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Lists roles (producer, caster, observer) in an event; sets up capture cards; checks audio levels; runs basic overlays.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Sets up basic scene switching and overlays; tests audio; coordinates player walk‑ons; switches camera feeds; manages stream latency.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Produces multi-camera feeds; designs stage lighting; integrates augmented‑reality graphics; manages replays; ensures redundancy.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates AR, remote talent, and redundant transmission paths; executes multi‑venue broadcasts; negotiates venue contracts; aligns with publisher technical standards.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Directs arena-scale events with 100k+ concurrent viewers; produces major international events; mentors production crews; publishes live‑event innovation studies.
