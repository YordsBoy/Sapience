proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Reads EPDs; defines system boundaries; identifies impact categories.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Uses LCA tools (SimaPro/OpenLCA); collects inventory data; calculates global‑warming potential (GWP).
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Performs sensitivity analysis; evaluates biogenic carbon; compares material options.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates LCA into BIM workflows; publishes material passports; optimises circularity.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads corporate LCA programmes; shapes product‑category rules; publishes LCA methodology advancements.
