label: Emergency Management & Continuity Operations
category: Technical
sector: government_public_admin
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Continuity"
  - "Emergency"
description: >
  Develops and tests continuity-of-operations plans (COOP) and incident-command structures in line with ISO 22301 Security & Resilience and FEMA National Incident Management System (NIMS) doctrines.
source_frameworks:
  - "ISO 22301:2019 Business Continuity Management"
  - "FEMA NIMS 2022 Update"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies essential functions, recovery time objectives (RTO), and RACI charts.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Drafts COOP annex; conducts tabletop drills; logs after-action items.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Coordinates cross-jurisdiction mutual-aid MoUs; integrates mass-alert tech; meets exercise-cycle targets.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Aligns COOP with ISO 22301 audit controls; embeds cyber-incident playbooks; evaluates cost-benefit.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads enterprise resilience program; influences national BCM standards; mentors emergency planners.
