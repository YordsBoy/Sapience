label: Crowd & Event Safety Management
category: Technical
sector: Public_Safety_Security
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Crowd Safety"
  - "Event Management"
description: Planning and managing crowd movement, egress, and emergency response for large events, following NFPA 101 Life Safety Code, NFPA 3000 Active-Shooter/Hostile-Event (ASHER) guidance, and ISO 20121 event sustainability.
source_frameworks:
  - "NFPA 101 Life-Safety Code"
  - "NFPA 3000 ASHER Standard"
  - "ISO 20121 Sustainable Events"
  - "Purple Guide Crowd Management"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Calculates occupancy loads and safe egress; identifies crushing risk; identifies pinch points; posts evacuation maps.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Drafts site plan; develops crowd‑management plans; sets occupant load; installs signage; positions stewards; monitors density.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements dynamic monitoring; uses crowd‑simulation tools; coordinates multi‑agency safety briefings; manages ingress/egress flows; triggers phased evacuation.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements real‑time crowd analytics; deploys emergency announcements; runs table-top & full-scale exercises; integrates UAS overwatch; audits safety KPIs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Certifies as crowd-safety manager; oversees mega‑event safety (stadia, festivals); shapes crowd‑management guidelines; publishes risk‑mitigation research.
