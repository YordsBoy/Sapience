proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Acknowledges guest issues; follows basic apology scripts; escalates unresolved problems.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Applies HEART steps; logs incidents in property‑management system (PMS); offers token gestures (amenity, discount).
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts root‑cause analysis; tracks service‑recovery scores; personalises recovery offers to guest profiles.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs property‑wide recovery playbooks; trains staff in empathetic communication such as LEARN (Listen, Empathise, Apologise, Resolve, Notify); integrates feedback loops into quality dashboards.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads multi‑property service‑excellence programs; benchmarks recovery ROI; publishes research on complaint‑resolution best practices.
