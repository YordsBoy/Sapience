## Purpose
A general-purpose cross-sector pathway guide for career changers, early-career professionals, and career explorers of any background. Organized by transferable cluster_tag domain — the competency clusters a person brings from their current work — each section identifies which sectors that domain bridges to most readily, what the transfer pathway looks like, and what CaliberPath services support the transition.

## How to Use This Guide
Identify which cluster_tag domains are strongest in the client's current competency profile (from their Career Compass UPLS assessment or intake). Navigate to those domain sections below to see which sectors those competencies transfer to, what roles are typical entry points, and what gaps to prioritize in a development plan.
