label: Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) Practices
category: Technical
sector: Core_IT_Cloud

cluster_tags:
  - "SRE"
  - "Reliability"
description: Applying Google‑originated Site Reliability Engineering principles—Service Level Objectives (SLOs), error budgets, and blameless post‑mortems—to maintain service reliability, scalability, and security in production systems.
source_frameworks:
  - "Google Site Reliability Engineering Book"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Describes SRE vs traditional ops; differentiates SLO, Service Level Indicator (SLI), and Service Level Agreement (SLA); participates in on‑call rotations.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Configures alert thresholds; writes run‑books; conducts root‑cause analysis with five‑whys method.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements graceful degradation; maintains error‑budget policies; integrates chaos‑engineering tools.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Automates capacity‑planning dashboards; designs multi‑region fail‑over; leads incident response, chaos testing, capacity planning, post‑mortem reviews.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Drives reliability culture; advises on org-level budgets/thresholds; aligns SRE roadmaps with business objectives; mentors SRE teams; publishes open‑source reliability‑tool frameworks.
