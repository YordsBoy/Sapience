proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies basic copyright requirements for third-party content; differentiates sync vs master use; lists common rights windows.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Drafts simple license agreements; records ISRC code; stores contracts; and maintains rights logs.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Negotiates usage terms, manages royalties, schedules renewals; resolves clearance flags; and handles takedown requests.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates rights database; automates alerts; negotiates multi-territory deals; oversees global rights portfolios, digital-rights management (DRM), and compliance audits.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads studio licensing; advises law makers; shapes IP strategy, cross-border licensing frameworks, and resolves complex infringement issues.
