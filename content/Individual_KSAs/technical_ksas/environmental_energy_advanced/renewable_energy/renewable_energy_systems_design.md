ksa_id: renewable_energy_systems_design
label: Renewable Energy Systems Design
category: Technical
sector: env_energy_advanced
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Renewable Energy"
  - "Design"
description: >
  Performs resource assessment, sizing, and electrical design for solar PV, wind, and hybrid plants; uses tools (PV*SOL, WindPRO) and codes (IEC 62548).
source_frameworks:
  - "IEC 62548:2023 Photovoltaic (PV) Arrays — Design Requirements (IEC proprietary standard — International Electrotechnical Commission; conceptual alignment only)"
  - "IEC 61724-1:2021 Photovoltaic System Performance — Part 1: Monitoring (IEC proprietary standard — International Electrotechnical Commission; conceptual alignment only)"
  - "IEC 61400 series Wind Energy Generation Systems (IEC proprietary standard series — International Electrotechnical Commission; conceptual alignment only)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Reads solar irradiance maps; identifies wind-class zones; names the primary components of a PV array and wind turbine system and explains their functions.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Uses solar energy simulation software (e.g., PVsyst, SAM, or equivalent); sizes inverters; calculates capacity factors.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts site assessments; designs electrical layouts, protections, and grounding; models wake effects; develops single-line diagrams.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates battery storage; performs grid-impact studies; optimizes levelized cost of energy (LCOE); secures permits.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads utility-scale project design; secures interconnection approvals; publishes renewable-design research.
