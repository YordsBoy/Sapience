source_frameworks:
  - "Aligned with OPM 'Interpersonal Skills,' 'Integrity/Honesty,' and 'Accountability' competency frameworks (public domain)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Follows basic workplace etiquette and dress code when reminded.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Consistently meets attendance and punctuality expectations; maintains courteous interactions.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Maintains composed, respectful conduct in client-facing and cross-organizational settings; handles confidential information responsibly by following applicable privacy and disclosure protocols.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Models professional conduct under pressure; mentors peers in workplace etiquette and ethics.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Shapes organizational policies on professionalism; leads initiatives to uphold and evolve ethical standards industry-wide.
