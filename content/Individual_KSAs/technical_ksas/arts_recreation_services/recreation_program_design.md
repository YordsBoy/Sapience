label: Recreation Program Design
category: Technical
sector: Arts_Recreation_Services
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags: ["Recreation Management","Program Development","Community Engagement"]
description: Planning, coordinating, and evaluating inclusive recreational programmes—sports, cultural activities, and community events—to enhance health, wellness, and social cohesion.
source_frameworks: ["National Recreation and Park Association (NRPA) Certified Park and Recreation Professional (CPRP) Outline","World Health Organization (WHO) Guidelines on Physical Activity"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Assists in set‑up of community sports leagues; completes participant registration forms.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Designs simple programme schedules; ensures equipment availability; implements basic risk assessments.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts needs assessments; manages volunteer staff; monitors budget and key performance indicators (KPIs).
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Develops evidence‑based programmes targeting diverse populations; secures sponsorships; applies Program Evaluation and Review Technique (PERT) for timeline control.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads regional recreation strategies; influences public policy; publishes research on recreation outcomes and best practices.
