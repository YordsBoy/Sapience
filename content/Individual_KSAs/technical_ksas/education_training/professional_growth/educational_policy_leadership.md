ksa_id: educational_policy_leadership
label: Educational Policy & Leadership
category: Technical
sector: Education_Training
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "Leadership"
  - "Policy"
description: >
  Develops, implements, and evaluates education policies, accreditation processes, and change-management initiatives that advance equitable student outcomes.
source_frameworks:
  - "Council for the Accreditation of Educator Preparation (CAEP) Standards"
  - "Kotter 8-Step Change Model"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Describes policy cycle; lists accrediting bodies.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Conducts policy scans; drafts position briefs; facilitates stakeholder forums.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Leads accreditation self-study; analyses compliance evidence; manages project timelines.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements change-management plans; allocates resources; monitors key performance indicators (KPIs).
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Shapes regional or national policy; guides system-wide reform; mentors emerging leaders.
