ksa_id: stage_production_management
label: Stage Production Management
category: Technical
sector: Arts_Recreation
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "Production"
  - "Stage Management"
description: >
  Coordinates all technical and artistic elements of live productions,
  producing cue sheets, call times, and safety briefings; liaises between
  directors, crew, and venue staff.
source_frameworks:
  - "USITT Backstage Handbook"
  - "OSHA Rigging Safety"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Follows basic backstage etiquette; identifies common stage gear.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Sets call boards; tracks props; calls simple cues.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Runs technical rehearsals; manages changeovers; mitigates hazards.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates automation, time-code; oversees touring logistics; manages budgets.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Directs multi-stage festivals; develops SOPs; mentors stage managers.
