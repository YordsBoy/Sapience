category: Technical
sector: Administrative_Support
horizon: core_2025

    - "Document Production"
    - "Collaboration"
description: >
  Produces high-quality documents (reports, slide decks, forms) using office
  suites; applies templates, accessibility, and version-control best practices
  within cloud collaboration platforms.
source_frameworks:
  - "Microsoft 365 Records Management"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Opens and saves documents; applies basic formatting.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Uses templates and styles; applies spell-check and basic layout.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Collaborates in real-time; inserts dynamic content (tables of contents, data links).
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Manages document versions; enforces accessibility standards; converts formats (PDF/A).
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Administers template libraries; trains teams; integrates document workflows with EDRMS.
