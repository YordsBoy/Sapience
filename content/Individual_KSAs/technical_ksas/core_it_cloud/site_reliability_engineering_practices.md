cluster_tags: ["SRE","DevOps","Reliability"]
description: Applying Google‑originated Site Reliability Engineering principles—Service Level Objectives (SLOs), error budgets, and blameless post‑mortems—to maintain service reliability, scalability, and security in production systems.
source_frameworks: ["Google Site Reliability Engineering Book"]
proficiency_levels:
- level: Awareness
indicator: Differentiates SLO, Service Level Indicator (SLI), and Service Level Agreement (SLA); participates in on‑call rotations.
- level: Basic
indicator: Configures alert thresholds; writes run‑books; conducts root‑cause analysis with five‑whys method.
- level: Intermediate
indicator: Implements graceful degradation; maintains error‑budget policies; integrates chaos‑engineering tools.
- level: Advanced
indicator: Automates capacity‑planning dashboards; designs multi‑region fail‑over; leads post‑mortem reviews.
- level: Expert
indicator: Aligns SRE roadmaps with business objectives; mentors SRE teams; publishes open‑source reliability‑tool frameworks.
