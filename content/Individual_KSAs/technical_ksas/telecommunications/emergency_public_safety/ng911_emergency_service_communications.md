proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Describes NG911 architecture; identifies ESInet components; explains location-based routing.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Configures Session Border Controllers (SBCs); validates GIS data; tests Text-to-911.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements Emergency Services IP Network (ESInet) redundancy; secures PSAP endpoints; enables text-to-911; implements call-queue policy; follows cyber-hardening checklist; monitors call-quality metrics.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Conducts cutover from legacy 911; integrates multimedia emergency services (video, sensor data); aligns with FirstNet priority services; integrates CAD and location-based routing; achieves 99.999 % uptime.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads statewide NG911 system migrations from legacy selective-routing to IP-based ESInet architecture; mentors PSAP engineers; advises state and federal regulators; publishes emergency-communications resilience studies.
