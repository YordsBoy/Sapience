ksa_id: sustainable_materials_life_cycle_assessment
label: Sustainable Materials & Life-Cycle Assessment (LCA)
category: Technical
sector: env_energy_advanced
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Sustainable Materials"
  - "Life Cycle"
description: >
  Evaluates material choices using embodied-carbon, toxicity, and circularity metrics; selects low-impact alternatives and supplier EPDs (environmental product declarations).
source_frameworks:
  - "EN 15804:2012+A2:2019 Sustainability of Construction Works — Environmental Product Declarations — Core Rules for the Product Category of Construction Products (CEN proprietary standard — European Committee for Standardization; conceptual alignment only)"
  - "ISO 14040:2006 / ISO 14044:2006 Environmental Management — Life Cycle Assessment — Principles, Framework, Requirements and Guidelines (ISO proprietary standard series; conceptual alignment only — CaliberPath does not assert reproduction rights)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Reads EPDs; defines system boundaries; identifies impact categories.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Compares materials via EPD databases; uses LCA tools (e.g., SimaPro, OpenLCA, or equivalent); collects inventory data; calculates global-warming potential (GWP).
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Performs sensitivity analysis; evaluates biogenic carbon; compares material options.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates LCA into BIM workflows; specifies bio-based or recycled materials at scale; publishes material passports; optimizes circularity.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Develops material-circularity targets; leads corporate LCA programs; shapes product-category rules; publishes LCA methodology advancements.
