proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies milestones; recognizes play types; selects safe toys; follows safe‑sleep practices.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Plans sensory play; establishes routines; sets safe play area; engages in parallel play; narrates actions; applies positive reinforcement.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Observes developmental delays; designs STEM play activities; uses sensory bins; adapts to special needs; logs observations; communicates with parents.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Adapts play for disabilities; integrates multicultural learning; integrates STEM toys; collaborates with therapists; tracks progress logs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs play-based curriculum; leads parent‑education workshops; influences childcare policy; publishes play‑based learning studies.
