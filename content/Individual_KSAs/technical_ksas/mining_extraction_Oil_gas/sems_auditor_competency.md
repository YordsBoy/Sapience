label: SEMS Auditor Competency (API RP 75)
category: Technical
sector: Mining_Extraction_Oil_Gas
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags: ["SEMS","Auditing","HSE Management"]
description: Planning and executing Safety‑ and Environmental‑Management System (SEMS) audits in accordance with American Petroleum Institute Recommended Practice 75 (API RP 75) to verify compliance, identify systemic hazards, and drive continuous improvement across offshore and onshore operations.
source_frameworks: ["API RP 75:2022 Safety & Environmental Management Systems","ISO 19011:2018 Auditing Management Systems"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies SEMS elements; understands audit terminology; reviews previous audit reports.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Develops audit checklists; conducts document reviews; records non‑conformities.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Leads field‑level interviews; samples work permits; grades findings by risk severity.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs audit‑sampling plans; validates corrective‑action progress; calculates audit performance metrics.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Oversees corporate SEMS audit programmes; mentors lead auditors; refines SEMS standards through industry workgroups.
