proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies potential sponsors; distinguishes CPM and CPA advertising pricing models; reads campaign briefs; inserts required disclosure hashtags; tracks basic deliverables.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Negotiates rate cards and disclosure requirements; calculates CPM rates; drafts scopes; schedules content calendars.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Negotiates usage rights; bundles multi‑platform packages; crafts integrated campaigns; aligns deliverables to KPIs; reports campaign analytics.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Manages multi-channel, multi-year exclusive partnerships; defends pricing with data; manages disputes and SLAs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads creator-brand portfolio strategy; leads talent‑agency negotiations; sets industry rate cards; publishes sponsorship ROI benchmarks.
