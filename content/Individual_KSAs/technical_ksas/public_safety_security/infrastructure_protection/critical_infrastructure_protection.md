label: Critical Infrastructure Protection (CIP)
category: Technical
sector: Public_Safety_Security
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "CIP"
  - "Risk Management"
description: Assessing vulnerabilities and implementing protective measures for lifeline sectors (energy, water, transportation) using DHS Risk Methodology and NIST 800‑82 for industrial control systems.
source_frameworks:
  - "DHS NIPP 2013"
  - "DHS Infrastructure Protection Plan"
  - "CISA Infrastructure Survey Tool"
  - "NIST SP 800‑82 ICS Security"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Lists 16 critical sectors; identifies critical assets; lists threat vectors; follows site‑security plans.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Completes IST survey; performs ASSESS/crosswalks; rates risk matrices; recommends basic mitigations.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts CARVER analyses; recommends target-hardening; aligns to CISA cross‑sector criteria; documents response plans; tracks metrics.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements resilience enhancements; integrates cyber‑physical assessments; manages protective‑measures budgets; coordinates sector‑specific agency (SSA) reporting; briefs leadership.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads regional CIP program; advises SSA policy; mentors analysts; informs national policy; publishes resilience frameworks.
