label: Climate Risk Modelling & Scenario Analysis
category: Technical
sector: env_energy_advanced
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "Climate Risk"
  - "Scenario Analysis"
description: >
  Modelling physical and transition climate risks using climate-economic models (GCAM, REMIND), network-based stress tests, downscaled climate data, and Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures (TCFD) frameworks.
source_frameworks:
  - "NGFS Climate Scenarios for Central Banks and Supervisors (Network for Greening the Financial System — central bank and supervisor coalition; publicly available)"
  - "TCFD Recommendations and guidance publications (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures — Financial Stability Board initiative; now integrated into IFRS S2 under ISSB; guidance documents publicly available)"
  - "IPCC CMIP6 (Coupled Model Intercomparison Project Phase 6) climate data protocols (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change / WCRP — intergovernmental; publicly available)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies RCP/SSP scenarios; maps hazards; reviews TCFD pillars.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Imports climate datasets; runs simple exposure analyses; interprets outputs; reports qualitative risks.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Performs sector vulnerability assessments; applies damage functions; quantifies transition pathways.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Builds stochastic scenario models; integrates financial stress tests; aligns disclosures with ISSB.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads enterprise climate-risk analytics; shapes reporting standards; publishes scenario-analysis research.
