  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Explains consultant–client confidentiality; recognises scope creep; drafts basic proposals.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Conducts stakeholder interviews; applies root‑cause analysis; adheres to disclosure requirements.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Designs value propositions; manages engagement economics; mitigates conflict‑of‑interest risks.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Leads multi‑stream transformation projects; negotiates statements of work (SOWs); oversees ethical‑risk assessments.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Mentors CMC candidates; sets global consulting standards; publishes consulting‑ethics research.
