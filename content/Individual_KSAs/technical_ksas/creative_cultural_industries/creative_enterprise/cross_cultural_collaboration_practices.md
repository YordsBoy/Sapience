proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies own cultural biases; practices active listening.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Adapts greetings, time norms, and feedback styles; clarifies roles.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Uses CQ assessments; mediates misunderstandings; establishes shared vocabulary and digital etiquette.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs collaboration charters; rotates leadership; aligns intercultural KPIs with project goals.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Coaches multinational teams; embeds DEI policy in creative workflows; speaks at global forums on collaboration.
