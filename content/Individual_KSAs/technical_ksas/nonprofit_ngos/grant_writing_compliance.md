description: Researching funding opportunities, crafting compelling grant proposals, and ensuring post‑award financial and programmatic compliance with donor regulations such as Uniform Guidance (2 CFR 200) and EU PRAG.
source_frameworks: ["Grants.gov Community Blog","Uniform Guidance 2 CFR 200"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Searches grant databases; summarises eligibility criteria; compiles required attachments.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Aligns project goals to donor priorities; drafts narratives; prepares line‑item budgets.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Coordinates multi‑partner proposals; manages compliance checklists; responds to donor clarifications.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Negotiates indirect‑cost rates; implements grant management systems; oversees financial reporting.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads grant‑acquisition strategy; audits compliance across portfolio; trains grant professionals.
