proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies high-risk tasks; lists assistive devices.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Applies gait belt; uses proper body mechanics; communicates steps.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Employs slide sheet; adjusts wheelchair; prevents shear injuries.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Operates sit-to-stand lift; tailors home layout; reduces caregiver strain.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Teaches workshops; audits safety incidents; advises home-modification grants.
