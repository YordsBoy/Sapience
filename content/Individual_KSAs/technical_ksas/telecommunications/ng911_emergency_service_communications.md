  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Describes NG911 architecture; identifies ESInet components; explains location‑based routing.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Configures Session Border Controllers (SBCs); validates GIS data; tests Text‑to‑911.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements Emergency Services IP Network (ESInet) redundancy; secures PSAP endpoints; monitors call‑quality metrics.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Conducts cutover from legacy 911; integrates multimedia emergency services (video, sensor data); aligns with FirstNet priority services.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads statewide NG911 deployments; advises regulators; publishes emergency‑comms resilience studies.
