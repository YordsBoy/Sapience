proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Reads nutrition labels; separates raw vs. cooked foods; sets portion sizes.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Creates weekly menus; shops with budget lists; checks food temperatures; uses portion plates; accounts for allergies.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Adjusts for diabetes/gluten‑free diets; adapts texture; budgets groceries; tracks hydration; batch cooks; reduces food waste.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Utilizes meal‑planning software; analyses macro/micro nutrients; manages garden‑to‑table sourcing; monitors weight/BMI; reduces food waste %.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads community nutrition classes; develops culturally inclusive recipes; mentors families; publishes household‑nutrition guides.
