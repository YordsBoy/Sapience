- level: Awareness
indicator: Identifies common fixture types; patches dimmers in a Digital Multiplex (DMX) universe; follows basic rigging safety.
- level: Basic
indicator: Builds simple cue stacks on entry‑level lighting consoles; adjusts colour temperature and intensity per designer notes.
- level: Intermediate
indicator: Creates multi‑scene presets and moving‑light effects; uses computer visualisation (What‑You‑See‑Is‑What‑You‑Get, WYSIWYG) software to pre‑cue shows.
- level: Advanced
indicator: Designs dynamic time‑coded sequences for concerts; integrates Art‑Net and Remote Device Management (RDM) networking for distributed control.
- level: Expert
indicator: Leads large‑scale multimedia lighting projects; mentors programmers; pioneers sustainable power management with laser and light‑emitting diode (LED) technologies.
