proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies trust drivers (competence, reliability).
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Joins local group; shares info; adheres to group rules.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Facilitates resource sharing; mediates disputes; logs contributions.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Structures rotating-savings group; tracks credit scores; organises training.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads federation; negotiates with buyers; mentors cluster leaders.
