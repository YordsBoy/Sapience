ksa_id: travel_expense_processing
label: Travel & Expense Processing
category: Technical
sector: administrative_support_services
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Travel"
  - "Expense Management"
description: >
  Arranges domestic and international travel, optimizing cost and compliance
  with corporate policies; reconciles expenses using digital expense-reporting
  systems and supports visa or insurance processes.
source_frameworks:
  - "IAAP Certified Administrative Professional (CAP) Body of Knowledge (commercial professional association — International Association of Administrative Professionals; conceptual alignment only)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies standard travel-booking terminology (fare classes, per-diem rates, preferred vendors, booking windows); collects and organizes receipts per corporate expense-reporting requirements.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Books flights/hotels through approved portals; submits expense reports accurately.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Manages complex itineraries (multi-city, multi-traveller); resolves issues with vendors; ensures policy compliance.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Negotiates corporate rates; analyses spend reports; streamlines visa/insurance workflows.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs travel-policy improvements; integrates expense systems with ERP; mentors travel-coordinators network-wide.
