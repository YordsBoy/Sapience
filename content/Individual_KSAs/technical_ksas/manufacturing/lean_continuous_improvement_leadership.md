ksa_id: lean_continuous_improvement_leadership
label: Lean Continuous‑Improvement Leadership
category: Technical
sector: Manufacturing
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags: ["Lean Manufacturing","Kaizen","Continuous Improvement"]
description: Leading enterprise‑wide lean transformations by applying Kaizen, value‑stream mapping, and problem‑solving methodologies to eliminate waste, boost productivity, and embed a culture of continuous improvement.
source_frameworks: ["SME Lean Certification Body of Knowledge","Toyota Production System (TPS) Principles","NAM Manufacturing Leadership Council Lean Playbook"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines the eight wastes; participates in 5S activities; uses simple root‑cause tools (5‑Whys).
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Maps current‑state value streams; calculates takt time; facilitates Kaizen events.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements pull systems; applies A3 problem‑solving; tracks Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE).
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Aligns policy deployment (hoshin kanri) with lean metrics; leads cross‑functional improvement teams; quantifies cost savings.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Drives enterprise lean strategy; mentors black‑belt practitioners; publishes lean‑transformation case studies.
