sector: Mining_Extraction_Oil_Gas

cluster_tags: ["Methane","LDAR","Emissions Management"]
description: Detecting, quantifying, and repairing fugitive methane emissions from upstream and midstream assets using optical gas‑imaging cameras, continuous‑monitoring sensors, and U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Quad OOOOa LDAR protocols to meet regulatory and investor requirements.
source_frameworks: ["EPA 40 CFR Part 60 Subpart OOOOa","OGMP 2.0 Methane Reporting Framework","ISO 14064‑1 Greenhouse‑Gas Accounting"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Explains greenhouse‑gas terminology; recognises common leak points (valves, flanges); follows basic OGI camera operations.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Conducts periodic leak surveys; classifies repair priorities; documents findings in LDAR database.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Utilises quantitative Optical Gas Imaging (qOGI); deploys continuous‑monitoring sensors; analyses emission‑rate calculations.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs facility‑wide LDAR programmes; integrates satellite/airborne data; verifies emission reductions for OGMP reporting.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads corporate methane‑intensity strategies; advises regulators on LDAR technology efficacy; publishes methane‑mitigation research.
