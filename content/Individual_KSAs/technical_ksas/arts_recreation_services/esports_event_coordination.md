proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Registers teams on tournament platforms; understands basic game‑rule sets; assists with streaming setup using Open Broadcaster Software (OBS).
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Schedules match brackets; coordinates player check‑in; moderates live chat; manages spectator seating.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Manages sponsor deliverables; integrates real‑time statistics overlays; resolves technical disputes.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Oversees multi‑title, multi‑region leagues; negotiates intellectual‑property licences; implements anti‑cheat monitoring.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Directs stadium‑scale international championships; shapes governance policies; secures broadcast rights across streaming networks.
