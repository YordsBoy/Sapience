description: Creating artistic soundscapes and operating front‑of‑house (FOH) mixing consoles to balance speech, music, and effects for theatrical, concert, and broadcast events, while maintaining acoustic clarity and audience comfort.
source_frameworks: ["United States Institute for Theatre Technology (USITT) Sound Documentation Recommendations"]
proficiency_levels:
- level: Awareness
indicator: Identifies microphone types and pickup patterns; assists with cable routing and stage plots.
- level: Basic
indicator: Sets gain structure on analog or digital consoles; employs equalisation to reduce feedback; follows cue sheets.
- level: Intermediate
indicator: Designs loudspeaker plots using acoustic‑modeling software; programs digital signal processing (DSP) scenes; manages radio‑frequency (RF) coordination.
- level: Advanced
indicator: Executes complex multitrack playback, surround‑sound sequences, and time‑aligned delay stacks; conducts sound checks with spectrum analysis.
- level: Expert
indicator: Leads international touring productions; develops immersive audio workflows (object‑based and spatial); mentors emerging sound designers.
