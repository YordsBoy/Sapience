description: >
  Utilises descriptive statistics and basic visualisation to interpret datasets; cleans and preprocesses data to support evidence-based decisions across business functions.
source_frameworks:
  - "ONET 15-2041.00"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Recognises common data types and simple charts; describes observable trends.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Uses a spreadsheet or BI tool to calculate summary statistics and plot charts.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Writes queries/scripts to aggregate data; applies measures such as variance,
      correlation, and basic hypothesis tests.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Conducts multivariate analyses, builds dashboards, and communicates insights to
      stakeholders.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs complex analytical studies, mentors others in statistical reasoning, and
      governs analytical best practices organisation-wide.
