proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Recognises unbanked demographic segments; lists barriers to access; explains KYC (Know Your Customer) basics.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Conducts field research; maps user journeys; drafts low‑cost fee structures.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Applies behavioural nudges; collaborates with mobile‑money operators; ensures data‑privacy compliance.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs tiered KYC regimes; secures fintech partnerships; measures social‑impact KPIs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads national‑level inclusion strategies; influences policy; publishes financial‑inclusion impact studies.
