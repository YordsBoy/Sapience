label: Participatory Budgeting & Civic Engagement
category: Technical
sector: Government_Public_Administration
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "Participation"
  - "Budgeting"
description: >
  Designs and implements participatory-budgeting (PB) cycles, facilitating citizen idea generation, deliberation, voting, and transparent allocation in accordance with International Association for Public Participation (IAP2)
  standards.
source_frameworks:
  - "IAP2 Spectrum of Public Participation"
  - "Participatory Budgeting Project Toolkit"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines PB stages; understands inclusion principles (gender, youth).
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Conducts outreach; moderates idea workshops; posts budget data online.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements secure e-voting; validates cost estimates; publishes progress dashboards.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates PB with annual budget cycle; analyses equity outcomes; scales to multiple districts.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Advises national PB policy; secures donor funding; mentors civic-engagement teams.
