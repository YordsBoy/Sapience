description: |
  Models token supply, incentive mechanisms, staking and burn schedules; analyses
  user-behavior data and DAO voting to forecast network health and protocol revenue.

  - "Token Taxonomy Framework v2.0 — open consortium framework — InterWork Alliance / Global Blockchain Business Council; publicly available"
  - "Electric Capital Developer Report — commercial venture firm research publication — Electric Capital; conceptual alignment only"

proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Calculates circulating versus total supply; distinguishes inflationary and deflationary models.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Runs agent-based simulations to test parameter changes; creates token-vesting schedules; models dilution scenarios; defines utility vs. governance vs. security tokens.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Performs discounted-cash-flow (DCF) for fee-burn models; analyses on-chain metrics like velocity and staking ratio; models dynamic emission curves; assesses protocol revenue sustainability.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Simulates game-theoretic behaviors; executes Monte Carlo stress tests; advises projects on regulatory token classifications; designs dynamic emission curves tied to KPIs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Publishes peer-reviewed token-economic frameworks; leads multi-chain token redesigns; consults central-bank digital-currency (CBDC) research teams.
