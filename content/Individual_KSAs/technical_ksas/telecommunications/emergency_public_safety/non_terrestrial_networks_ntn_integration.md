source_frameworks:
  - "3GPP TS 38.821 — Solutions for NR to support Non-Terrestrial Networks (3GPP standards body; publicly available)"
  - "3GPP TS 23.122 Release 17 — Non-Access-Stratum functions related to NTN (3GPP standards body; publicly available)"
  - "ETSI TR 103 611 — Satellite Earth Stations and Systems (SES): Seamless integration of satellite and/or HAPS systems into 5G and related architecture options (European Telecommunications Standards Institute; publicly available)"
  - "ITU-R S.2460 — Propagation prediction methods for satellite communication systems (International Telecommunication Union; publicly available)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines NTN types; recognizes propagation delays; lists use cases.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Configures satellite backhaul; simulates link budgets; tests attach; measures SNR; tracks Doppler shifts.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements hybrid-handovers; tunes HARQ process; compensates Doppler; aligns RRC timers; optimizes QoS mapping; tests NB-IoT NTN waveforms.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs multi-orbit architectures; manages spectrum coordination; implements slice continuity across ground-space domains; verifies roaming; meets QoS class requirements; analyzes capacity trade-offs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads NTN-5G convergence programs; contributes to 3GPP NTN standards; publishes NTN integration research.
