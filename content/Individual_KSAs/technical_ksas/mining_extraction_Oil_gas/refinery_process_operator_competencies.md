description: Operating distillation, conversion, and treatment units in petroleum refineries, applying process‑safety management, advanced control systems, and turnaround planning to maximise throughput and comply with environmental regulations.
source_frameworks: ["API Process Safety Site Assessment Program (PSSAP)","Energy Institute (EI) Process Operator Competency Framework"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies major refinery units; reads process flow diagrams (PFDs); follows permit‑to‑work procedures.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Monitors control‑room parameters; conducts field rounds; logs unit performance data.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Adjusts advanced process controls (APC); troubleshoots unit upsets; tracks key performance indicators (KPIs).
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Plans shutdown/start‑up sequences; manages catalyst changes; coordinates flare minimisation strategies.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads multi‑unit optimisation; oversees turnaround execution; mentors operators; contributes to process‑safety guidelines.
