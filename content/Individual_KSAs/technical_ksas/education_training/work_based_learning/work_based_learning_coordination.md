---
ksa_id: work_based_learning_coordination
label: Work-Based Learning Coordination
category: Technical
sector: Education_Training
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "Work-Based Learning"
  - "Partnerships"
description: >
  Designs, coordinates, and evaluates apprenticeships, internships, and
  cooperative education aligned with industry standards, labor regulations, and
  learner outcomes.
source_frameworks:
  - "U.S. Department of Labor Apprenticeship Toolkit"
  - "European Alliance for Apprenticeships Quality Framework"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines placement types; identifies legal and safety requirements.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Matches learners to hosts; drafts learning agreements; orients supervisors.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Monitors progress via site visits; assesses competencies; resolves workplace issues.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Scales multi-sector partnerships; aligns tax incentives; tracks return-on-investment (ROI).
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads regional work-based-learning strategy; secures grants; publishes best-practice toolkits.
