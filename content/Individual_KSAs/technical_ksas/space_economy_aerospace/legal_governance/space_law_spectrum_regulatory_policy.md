    indicator: Identifies major space treaties; recognises frequency bands; notes licensing timelines.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Prepares ITU advance publication information (API); submits orbital‑slot filings; drafts part 25 license applications; identifies ECC/NTIA coordination steps.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Coordinates spectrum with administrations; analyses debris‑mitigation plan compliance; interprets export‑control rules (ITAR/EAR); manages TCO (technology-control) plan.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Leads regulatory negotiations; negotiates cross-border spectrum; manages filing portfolios; advises on liability and insurance terms.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Shapes space‑policy reforms; represents operators at ITU World Radiocommunication Conferences (WRC); publishes legal analyses.
