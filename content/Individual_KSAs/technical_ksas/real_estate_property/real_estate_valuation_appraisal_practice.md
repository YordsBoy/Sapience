proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Collects comparable sales; measures gross living area (GLA); confirms zoning.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Calculates cost new and depreciation; prepares income‑capitalisation summaries; writes restricted appraisal reports.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Performs discounted‑cash‑flow (DCF) analysis; reconciles multiple valuation approaches; adjusts for market conditions.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Conducts highest‑and‑best‑use studies; defends appraisals in litigation; complies with USPAP record‑keeping.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads complex portfolio valuations; mentors trainee appraisers; contributes to valuation standards.
