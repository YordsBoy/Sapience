description: Designing solar photovoltaic (PV), onshore wind, and hybrid renewable systems, performing resource assessment, energy yield modelling, and interconnection studies.

proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Reads solar irradiance maps; identifies wind‑class zones; understands basic components.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Uses PVsyst/SAM models; sizes inverters; calculates capacity factors.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts site assessments; models wake effects; develops single‑line diagrams.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates battery storage; performs grid‑impact studies; optimises levelised cost of energy (LCOE).
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads utility‑scale project design; secures interconnection approvals; publishes renewable‑design research.
