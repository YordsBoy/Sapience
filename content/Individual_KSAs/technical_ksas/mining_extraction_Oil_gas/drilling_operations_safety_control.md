description: Planning and executing drilling programmes while maintaining well control, applying blowout‑prevention (BOP) equipment, kick detection, and IWCF Level 4 well‑control procedures to ensure personnel and environmental safety.
source_frameworks: ["IWCF Well Control Level 3 & 4," "American Petroleum Institute (API) RP 59 Recommended Practice for Well‑Control Operations"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Reads mud‑logging charts; identifies early kick indicators; follows standpipe‑pressure guidelines.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Calculates kill‑mud weight; performs drill‑floor safety briefings; inspects BOP stack components.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Executes well‑kill operations (driller’s and engineer’s methods); manages drilling fluids rheology; logs deviation surveys.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs managed‑pressure drilling (MPD) plans; integrates real‑time downhole sensors; leads hazard‑identification (HAZID) workshops.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Oversees ultra‑deepwater drilling campaigns; sets corporate well‑control standards; mentors IWCF certification candidates.
