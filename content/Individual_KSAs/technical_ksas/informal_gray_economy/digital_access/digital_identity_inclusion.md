label: Digital Identity Inclusion for Informal Workers
category: Technical
sector: Informal_Gray_Economy
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "Digital ID"
  - "Inclusion"
description: >
  Obtains and uses foundational or functional digital identity—SIM registration, mobile e-KYC—aligned with World Bank Identification for Development (ID4D) principles to access finance and formal services.
source_frameworks:
  - "World Bank ID4D Principles"
  - "GSMA Digital Identity for the Unregistered"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Explains benefits of digital ID; lists enrolment options.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Submits biometric data; secures One-Time Password (OTP); stores credentials safely.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Links ID to mobile money; retrieves e-records; updates status.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Uses self-sovereign ID wallet; accesses cross-border services; educates peers.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Advocates inclusive ID policy; mentors NGOs; contributes to interoperability pilots.
