category: Technical

  - "Business"
  - "Culture"
description: Launching and scaling ventures in the arts and cultural sector by leveraging creative assets, intellectual property, and community networks to generate social and economic value.
source_frameworks: 
  - "Organisation for Economic Co‑operation and Development (OECD) Cultural Entrepreneurship Study"
  - "UNESCO Transcultura Cultural-Entrepreneurship Programme"
  - "UNCTAD Creative-Economy Outlook 2022"  
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Conducts basic market research; identifies cultural value propositions; drafts business‑model canvas.
  - level: Basic
    indicator:  Drafts mission, simple marketing plans and value proposition, and minimal viable product; registers legal entities; secures micro‑funding or grants.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Secures seed funding or grants; measures social-cultural impact (e.g., jobs, heritage value; negotiates licensing of creative content; structures revenue‑sharing agreements; manages crowdfunding campaigns.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Forms public-private partnerships; expands to export markets; Scales ventures internationally; implements impact‑measurement frameworks; embeds diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) goals.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Advises government cultural‑policy bodies; invests in creative‑startup portfolios; publishes research on cultural‑economy trends; scales multi-country operations; mentors new cultural founders.
