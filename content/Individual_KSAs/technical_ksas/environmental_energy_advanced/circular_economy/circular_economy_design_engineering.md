label: Circular Economy Design Engineering
category: Technical
sector: Env_Energy_Advanced
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "Circular Economy"
  - "Design"
description: >
  Applies cradle-to-cradle principles to product and infrastructure design; prioritises reuse, remanufacturing, recycling, and regenerative materials.
source_frameworks:
  - "Ellen MacArthur Foundation CE Principles"
  - "Ellen MacArthur Foundation Circular Design Guide"
  - "ISO 59010:2023 Circular Economy Guidelines"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines linear vs circular models; identifies waste streams; calculates recyclability; recognises take‑back schemes.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Applies design‑for‑disassembly principles; selects recyclable materials; designs for disassembly; analyses end‑of‑life pathways.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Develops product‑as‑service models; quantifies circularity indicators; conducts reverse‑logistics studies.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates circularity into product development stage‑gates; measures economic returns; secures certification (Cradle to Cradle).
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads systemic CE transitions at city or sector scale and/or multi‑product circular portfolios; shapes circular‑economy standards; publishes design‑engineering research.
