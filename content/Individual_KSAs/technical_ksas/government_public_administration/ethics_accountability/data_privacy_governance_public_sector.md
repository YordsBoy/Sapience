label: Data-Privacy Governance in the Public Sector
category: Technical
sector: Government_Public_Administration
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags:
  - "Privacy"
  - "Governance"
description: >
  Establishes public-sector data-privacy programs aligned with General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles, National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Privacy Framework, and Freedom of Information laws.
source_frameworks:
  - "NIST Privacy Framework 1.0"
  - "EU General Data Protection Regulation"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies personal data categories; defines lawful bases for processing.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Produces privacy notices; performs records-of-processing; responds to subject-access requests.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts privacy-impact assessments; embeds privacy-by-design controls.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements data-minimisation and retention schedules; aligns privacy with FOI transparency.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads enterprise privacy-governance board; harmonises multinational requirements; mentors DPOs.
