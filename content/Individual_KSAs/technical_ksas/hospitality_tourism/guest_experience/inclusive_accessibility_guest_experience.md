ksa_id: inclusive_accessibility_guest_experience
label: Inclusive & Accessibility Guest Experience
category: Technical
sector: hospitality_tourism
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Accessibility"
  - "Inclusion"
description: >
  Designs barrier-free stays following Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.2), and International Association of Accessibility Professionals (IAAP) best practices.
source_frameworks:
  - "ADA Title III Hospitality Guidance — U.S. government — DOJ; public domain"
  - "W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 — international standards consortium — W3C; publicly available"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies common barriers; lists room ADA features.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Provides tactile signage; ensures alt-text on website; records guest preferences.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements room-ready accessibility kits; trains staff in inclusive language.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Audits facilities via IAAP standards; integrates accessibility in design & marketing.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads universal-design renovations; obtains third-party certification; mentors teams.
