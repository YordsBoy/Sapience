category: Technical
sector: Customer_Experience_Service
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Journey Mapping"
  - "Touchpoints"
description: >
  Visualises end-to-end customer journeys, mapping emotions and touchpoints,
  then prioritises improvements based on effort/impact analysis and real-world
  analytics.
source_frameworks:
  - "Qualtrics Journey-Mapping Guide"
  - "UXPressia Journey-Mapping Framework"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Lists journey stages; notes channels and personas.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Drafts current-state map; annotates pain points; validates with frontline staff.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Integrates quantitative data (conversion, dwell time); calculates opportunity sizing.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs future-state maps; runs service-design sprints; aligns owners to each touchpoint.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Embeds journey maps in product roadmap; automates real-time journey analytics; mentors CX strategists.
