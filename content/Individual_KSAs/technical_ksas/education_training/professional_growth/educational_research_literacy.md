---
ksa_id: educational_research_literacy
label: Educational Research Literacy
category: Technical
sector: Education_Training
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "Research"
  - "Evidence"
description: >
  Critically reads, interprets, and applies educational research following
  American Educational Research Association (AERA) and evidence-based practice
  standards to enhance pedagogy.
source_frameworks:
  - "AERA Standards for Reporting Research"
  - "What Works Clearinghouse (WWC) Procedures"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines quantitative vs qualitative study; locates abstract and findings.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Evaluates sample size, validity, and reliability; summarises implications.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts literature review; calculates effect size; applies findings to course revision.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs action-research study; secures ethical approval; shares results at conferences.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads institutional research committee; publishes peer-reviewed articles; mentors scholar-practitioners.
---
