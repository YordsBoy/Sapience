label: Lifecycle GHG Accounting & Life‑Cycle Assessment (LCA)
category: Technical
sector: Environmental_Advanced_Energy
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags: ["GHG Accounting","LCA","Sustainability Metrics"]
description: Quantifying greenhouse‑gas emissions across product and process life‑cycles using standards‑based methods (ISO 14040 series, GHG Protocol) and communicating results for sustainability reporting and decision‑making.
source_frameworks: ["ISO 14044:2020 Life‑Cycle Assessment","GHG Protocol Product Standard"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies scope 1, 2, 3 emissions; gathers activity data; utilises emission‑factor databases.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Builds simple process‑flow diagrams; calculates cradle‑to‑gate emissions; documents data quality.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts sensitivity analyses; applies allocation methods; uses LCA software (SimaPro, OpenLCA).
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates consequential LCA; validates results with third‑party review; aligns disclosures to IFRS S2.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads corporate‑wide GHG inventories; develops sector‑specific guidance; publishes peer‑reviewed LCA studies.
