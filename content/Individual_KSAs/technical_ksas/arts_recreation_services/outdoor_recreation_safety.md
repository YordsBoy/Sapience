proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies basic hazard symbols; follows Leave No Trace principles; assists in equipment checks.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Conducts pre‑trip briefings; performs primary surveys using Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) protocols; carries emergency communication devices.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Develops activity‑specific risk assessments; implements lightning and wildlife‑encounter protocols; documents incident reports.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs comprehensive Emergency Action Plans (EAPs); trains staff in swift‑water rescue and navigation; coordinates with local search‑and‑rescue teams.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Establishes organisation‑wide safety standards; audits multi‑site operations; advises policymakers on outdoor‑recreation regulations.
