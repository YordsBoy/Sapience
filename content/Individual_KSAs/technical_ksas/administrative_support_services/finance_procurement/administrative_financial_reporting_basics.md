description: >
  Supports financial reporting and budget-monitoring functions at the administrative level; prepares expense reports, interprets basic profit-and-loss summaries, reconciles invoices to purchase orders, and ensures accurate coding of transactions in financial systems in compliance with organizational policy and relevant accounting standards.
source_frameworks:

proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies the purpose of common financial documents (expense reports, purchase orders, invoices, budget summaries) and explains how they relate to each other in a standard procure-to-pay cycle.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Completes expense reports accurately; matches invoices to purchase orders; applies correct cost-center and general-ledger codes; submits within policy deadlines.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Reconciles departmental accounts against budget reports; identifies and resolves coding discrepancies; prepares basic monthly spend summaries and flags variances for manager review.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Maintains accrual and prepaid schedules; supports month-end close activities; liaises with finance team to resolve audit queries; improves reporting accuracy through process documentation and data-validation controls.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs administrative financial reporting workflows and internal controls; trains staff on financial compliance procedures; leads continuous-improvement projects that reduce close-cycle time and error rate across the administrative function.
