  - level: Basic

  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Executes off‑target analysis (TIDE); applies personal protective equipment (PPE); maintains Electronic Lab Notebooks (ELN).
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Optimises CRISPR efficiency via ribonucleoprotein (RNP) delivery; conducts risk assessments; oversees IBC submissions.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads multi‐gene editing programs; shapes genome‐editing biosafety policy; publishes high‑impact protocols.
