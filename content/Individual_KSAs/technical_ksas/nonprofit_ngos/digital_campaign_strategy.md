  - level: Awareness

  - level: Basic
    indicator: Segments mailing lists; sets up landing pages; runs A/B tests on subject lines.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Utilises pixel tracking; optimises conversion funnels; employs influencer outreach.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates marketing automation; applies predictive lead‑scoring; aligns digital KPIs with fundraising goals.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Architects omni‑channel journeys; manages six‑figure ad grants; publishes digital‑campaign benchmark studies.
