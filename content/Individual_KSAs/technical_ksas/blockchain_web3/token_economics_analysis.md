- level: Awareness
indicator: Calculates circulating versus total supply; distinguishes inflationary and deflationary models.
- level: Basic
indicator: Creates token‑vesting schedules; models dilution scenarios; defines utility vs. governance vs. security tokens.
- level: Intermediate
indicator: Performs discounted‑cash‑flow (DCF) for fee‑burn models; analyses on‑chain metrics like velocity and staking ratio.
- level: Advanced
indicator: Simulates game‑theoretic behaviours; executes Monte Carlo stress tests; advises projects on regulatory token classifications.
- level: Expert
indicator: Publishes peer‑reviewed token‑economic frameworks; leads multi‑chain token redesigns; consults central‑bank digital‑currency (CBDC) research teams.
