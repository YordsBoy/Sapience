  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies major BESS components; reads State of Charge (SoC) displays; follows lock‑out/tag‑out procedures.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Performs routine inspections; tracks round‑trip efficiency; logs thermal‑management data.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Executes charge/discharge schedules via Energy Management System (EMS); analyses degradation trends; responds to alarms.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Optimises dispatch against wholesale‑market signals; calibrates Battery Management System (BMS) parameters; implements fire‑suppression protocols.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs fleet‑wide optimisation strategies; integrates second‑life batteries; advises regulators on storage interconnection standards.
