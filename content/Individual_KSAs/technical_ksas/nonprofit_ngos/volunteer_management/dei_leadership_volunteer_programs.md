label: Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) Leadership in Volunteer Programs
category: Technical
sector: Nonprofit_NGOs
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "DEI"
  - "Volunteer Management"
description: >
  Designs inclusive volunteer recruitment, onboarding, and retention strategies following Race Equity and Inclusion (REI) Action Guide and UN Volunteer diversity standards.
source_frameworks:
  - "REI Action Guide (Annie E. Casey Foundation)"
  - "UNV Volunteer Diversity Guidelines"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines equity vs equality; tracks demographics.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Removes biased language; offers accessibility accommodations; collects feedback.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Sets DEI goals; trains coordinators; publishes dashboard.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates DEI into succession planning; measures impact on retention.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Advises board on DEI; mentors sector networks; presents at conferences.
