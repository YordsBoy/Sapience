sector: Informal_Gray_Economy
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "Accounting"
  - "Cash Basis"
description: >
  Maintains simplified cash-basis books—receipts, payments, petty-cash—aligned with International Federation of Accountants (IFAC) SME guidelines to support micro-enterprise decision-making.
source_frameworks:
  - "IFAC SME Accounting Guide"
  - "OECD Non-Observed Economy Measurement"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Distinguishes cash vs accrual; identifies basic records (sales, expenses).
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Uses columnar book or spreadsheet; reconciles cash daily; retains receipts.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Calculates profit, cash flow; separates personal and business funds; prepares simple tax worksheet.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements mobile-money sync; analyses seasonal trends; reduces cash-leakage risks.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Trains peer entrepreneurs; migrates to hybrid accrual when scaling; liaises with micro-financiers.
