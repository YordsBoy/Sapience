description: Designing and conducting rigorous scientific studies while upholding research‑integrity principles—authorship, data transparency, avoidance of fabrication—per OECD *Frascati Manual* and Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) guidelines.
source_frameworks:
  - "CONSORT 2010 Statement"
  - "FAIR Data Principles (GO FAIR)"
  - "OECD Frascati Manual 2015","COPE Core Practices"
  - "National Academies Research Integrity Report 2023"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Formulates testable hypotheses; ; identifies bias types; distinguishes qualitative vs. quantitative methods; cites sources properly.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Designs sampling strategies; selects appropriate design; preregisters protocol; manages data‑collection protocols; registers studies on open registries.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Applies statistical‑power analysis; applies randomisation; maintains version‑controlled data; conducts peer debriefs.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements preregistration; performs reproducibility checks; passes peer-review integrity checks; shares FAIR dataset; addresses conflicts of interest.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Chairs institutional review boards (IRBs); leads meta‑research initiatives; shapes national integrity policies.
