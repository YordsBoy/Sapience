ksa_id: experiential_learning_facilitation
label: Experiential Learning Facilitation
category: Technical
sector: Education_Training
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Experiential"
  - "Facilitation"
description: >
  Guides learners through Kolb’s Experiential Learning Cycle—concrete experience, reflective observation, abstract conceptualisation, active experimentation—using simulations, fieldwork, and service-learning.
source_frameworks:
  - "Kolb Experiential Learning Theory (ELT)"
  - "National Society for Experiential Education (NSEE) Principles"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: States the four ELT stages; identifies reflection prompts.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Organises small-scale simulations; debriefs using reflective-journals.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Coordinates field placements; develops assessment rubrics; connects theory to practice.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Manages multi-stakeholder service-learning projects; measures impact on competencies.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Researches experiential-learning outcomes; consults across departments; secures partnerships.
