description: >
  Handles colourants, relaxers, and disinfectants under OSHA Hazard Communication (29 CFR 1910.1200), monitoring formaldehyde limits and proper Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).
source_frameworks:
  - "OSHA Hair-Salon Chemical Hazard Alert"
  - "NIOSH PPE Selection Guide"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Reads Safety Data Sheet (SDS); identifies hazard pictograms.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Mixes products in ventilated area; dons gloves & goggles.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Calculates time-weighted exposure; stores chemicals securely.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Installs source-capture ventilation; trains team; maintains exposure logs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Advises industry panels; publishes safety protocol; mentors educators.
