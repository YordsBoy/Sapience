description: Applying European Cooperation for Space Standardization (ECSS) and NASA Systems Engineering Handbook processes to define requirements, architect spacecraft subsystems, manage verification/validation, and control configuration across the mission lifecycle.
source_frameworks: ["NASA SP‑20210023201 Systems Engineering Handbook Rev 3","ECSS‑E‑ST‑10C Space Engineering – System Engineering General Requirements"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Lists spacecraft subsystems; interprets requirement identifiers; participates in design reviews.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Crafts requirement traceability matrices; allocates mass/power budgets; updates interface control documents (ICDs).
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Performs trade‑studies; supports hardware‑in‑the‑loop testing; manages change requests via configuration‑control boards (CCBs).
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Leads system requirements reviews (SRRs) and preliminary design reviews (PDRs); manages risk burn‑down; integrates cross‑disciplinary models.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Directs end‑to‑end spacecraft architectures; mentors systems engineers; contributes to ECSS standards.
