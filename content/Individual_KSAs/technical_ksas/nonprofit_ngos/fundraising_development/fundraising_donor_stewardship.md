label: Fundraising & Donor Stewardship
category: Technical
sector: nonprofit_ngos
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Fundraising"
  - "Stewardship"
description: Planning and executing diversified fundraising strategies—annual giving, major gifts, corporate sponsorships—while cultivating donor relationships, acknowledging gifts, and retaining supporters according to Association of Fundraising Professionals (AFP) best practices.
source_frameworks:
  - "AFP Code of Ethical Standards"
  - "AFP Ethical Fundraising Principles"
  - "Case for Support Framework (CASE)"
  - "CFRE Exam Content Outline"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Explains fundraising terminology; follows gift‑acknowledgement timelines; updates donor records; Lists gift pyramid; identifies prospect research sources.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Writes case statement; schedules cultivation calls; logs pledges; drafts solicitation letters; segments donor lists; tracks response rates.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Coordinates gift agreements; designs campaign dashboard; designs multi‑channel campaigns; conducts donor‑advised fund outreach; recognises donors; analyses donor‑lifecycle metrics.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Leads quiet phase; manages major‑gift pipelines; deploys donor‑relationship‑management (DRM) software; oversees stewardship events.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads integrated capital campaigns; mentors fundraising teams; publishes donor‑engagement research.
