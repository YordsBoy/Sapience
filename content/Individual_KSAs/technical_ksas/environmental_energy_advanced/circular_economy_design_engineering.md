label: Circular Economy Design Engineering
category: Technical
sector: Environmental_Advanced_Energy
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags: ["Climate Engineering","Circular Economy","Design"]
description: Applying design‑for‑disassembly, material looping, and product‑as‑a‑service models to minimise waste and maximise resource efficiency, using Ellen MacArthur Foundation (EMF) frameworks.
source_frameworks: ["Ellen MacArthur Foundation Circular Design Guide","ISO 59010:2023 Circular Economy Guidelines"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies waste streams; calculates recyclability; recognises take‑back schemes.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Applies design‑for‑disassembly principles; selects recyclable materials; analyses end‑of‑life pathways.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Develops product‑as‑service models; quantifies circularity indicators; conducts reverse‑logistics studies.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates circularity into product development stage‑gates; measures economic returns; secures certification (Cradle to Cradle).
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads multi‑product circular portfolios; shapes circular‑economy standards; publishes design‑engineering research.
