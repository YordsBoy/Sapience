proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Recognises common XR gestures; explains comfort vs. presence trade‑offs; uses default interaction prefabs.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Designs ray‑casting selection; applies safe‑zone guidelines; conducts basic VR sickness checks.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Implements hand‑tracking and haptic feedback; runs hallway‑usability tests; iterates based on System Usability Scale (SUS).
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs multi‑modal interactions (voice + gesture); applies Fitts’s Law in 3D; integrates accessibility features for low‑vision users.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads enterprise UX standards for XR; publishes research on spatial‑interaction metrics; consults on ISO/IEC XR usability standards.
