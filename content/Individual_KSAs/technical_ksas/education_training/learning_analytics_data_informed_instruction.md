label: Learning Analytics & Data‑Informed Instruction
category: Technical
sector: Education_Training
horizon: emerging
cluster_tags: ["Learning Analytics","Data‑Driven Teaching","Educational Data Mining"]
description: Collecting, analysing, and interpreting learner‑interaction data to personalise instruction, predict at‑risk students, and improve pedagogical decisions at classroom and institutional levels.
source_frameworks: ["Society for Learning Analytics Research (SoLAR) Practitioner Guide"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Accesses LMS gradebook; identifies basic engagement metrics; recognises privacy considerations.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Generates learning‑analytics dashboards; flags low‑participation patterns; implements simple interventions.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Utilises predictive‑risk models; correlates engagement with achievement; applies anonymisation protocols.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs randomised‑controlled trials; integrates multi‑source datasets; measures intervention efficacy.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads institution‑wide learning‑analytics programmes; publishes open datasets; advises on ethical AI in education.
