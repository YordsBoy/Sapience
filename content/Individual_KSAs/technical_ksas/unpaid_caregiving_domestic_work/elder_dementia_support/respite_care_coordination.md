description: Arranging temporary relief care, navigating service providers, and leveraging community resources to prevent caregiver burnout.
source_frameworks:
  - "ARCH National Respite Network Standards"
  - "Zarit Respite Outcomes Meta-analysis"
  - "Family Caregiver Alliance Best Practices"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Recognises burnout signs; lists respite options; identifies eligibility; gathers contact info.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Contacts provider; schedules respite sessions; completes intake forms; communicates care instructions; schedules break.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Evaluates provider credentials; secures funding assistance; maintains care logs.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Develops contingency plans; coordinates multi‑provider schedules; evaluates QoL metrics; prevents burnout; tracks relief outcomes.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads regional respite networks; secures grant funding; leads community respite program; mentors carers; publishes caregiver‑relief research.
