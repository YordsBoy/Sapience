    indicator: Forecasts cost vs. traffic; rightsizes virtual machines; automates savings plans.

    indicator: Implements chargeback/showback models; integrates FinOps dashboards with OSS/BSS; benchmarks cost per subscriber.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads telco‑cloud cost‑governance boards; negotiates licence optimisations; publishes FinOps case studies.
