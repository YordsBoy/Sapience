proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Configures sequence settings in NLE software; identifies common codec types and their use cases; cuts clips and adds basic transitions.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Syncs audio; applies basic color correction; cuts interview A-roll; adds b-roll; exports to common codecs.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Colour-matches cameras; syncs audio; delivers PRORES master; executes multi-camera edits, motion graphics, and platform-specific aspect ratios.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Leads post-production pipelines; color-grades with LUTs; collaborates on VFX integration; grades HDR10; collaborates in Team Projects; inserts captions per CEA-608/708.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Architects studio workflows, HDR mastering, and adaptive streaming optimization; mentors editors; writes workflow white paper.
