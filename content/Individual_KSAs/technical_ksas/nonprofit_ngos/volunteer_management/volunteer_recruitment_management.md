description: Sourcing, onboarding, supervising, and recognizing volunteers to maximise mission impact and retention, following Council for Certification in Volunteer Administration (CCVA) competencies.
source_frameworks:
  - "Points of Light Service Enterprise program standards (nonprofit organization; conceptual alignment only)"
  - "VolunteerPro volunteer screening and management resources (vendor documentation — VolunteerPro commercial platform; conceptual alignment only — equivalent volunteer management platform screening resources may demonstrate competency)"
  - "CCVA Volunteer Administration Competency Framework (professional certification body — Council for Certification in Volunteer Administration; conceptual alignment only)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Defines role descriptions; posts call-outs; posts volunteer opportunities; screens basic applications; conducts orientation sessions.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Checks references; logs agreement; matches volunteer skills to roles; tracks hours; provides supervision and feedback.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Schedules orientation; tracks hours; designs recognition programs; manages risk and liability; collects volunteer satisfaction data.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates volunteers into program logic models; develops leadership pathways; analyses retention metrics; customises incentives; integrates DEI goals.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads large volunteer corps; directs enterprise volunteer strategies; secures corporate volunteer partnerships; publishes volunteer-management research.
