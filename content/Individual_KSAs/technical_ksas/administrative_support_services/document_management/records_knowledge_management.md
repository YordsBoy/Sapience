ksa_id: records_knowledge_management
label: Records & Knowledge Management
category: Technical
sector: Administrative_Support
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags:
  - "Knowledge Management"
  - "Taxonomy"
description: >
  Curates organisational knowledge bases; designs taxonomies, tags documents,
  and facilitates knowledge-sharing platforms (wikis, intranets) to reduce
  information silos and speed onboarding.
source_frameworks:
  - "ISO 30401 Knowledge Management Systems"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Searches intranet; adds simple wiki entries.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Tags documents; updates FAQs; promotes use of knowledge base.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Develops taxonomy; analyses usage metrics; removes redundant/outdated info.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates KM with EDRMS; facilitates communities of practice; trains staff on knowledge capture.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads KM strategy; measures ROI; aligns KM processes with ISO 30401.
