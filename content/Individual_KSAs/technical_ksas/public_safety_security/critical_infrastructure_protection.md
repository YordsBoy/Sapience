label: Critical Infrastructure Protection (CIP)
category: Technical
sector: Public_Safety_Security
horizon: perennial
cluster_tags: ["CIP","Risk Assessment","DHS"]
description: Assessing vulnerabilities and implementing protective measures for lifeline sectors (energy, water, transportation) using DHS Risk Methodology and NIST 800‑82 for industrial control systems.
source_frameworks: ["DHS Infrastructure Protection Plan","NIST SP 800‑82 ICS Security"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies critical assets; lists threat vectors; follows site‑security plans.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Performs ASSESS/crosswalks; rates risk matrices; recommends basic mitigations.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Conducts CARVER analyses; aligns to CISA cross‑sector criteria; documents response plans.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Integrates cyber‑physical assessments; manages protective‑measures budgets; coordinates sector‑specific agency (SSA) reporting.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads regional CIP programmes; advises SSA policy; publishes resilience frameworks.
