label: ICT‑Integrated Teaching Practices
category: Technical
sector: Education_Training
horizon: core_2025
cluster_tags: ["ICT in Education","Digital Pedagogy","EdTech"]
description: Applying Information and Communication Technology (ICT) tools—learning‑management systems, collaborative apps, digital content—to design and facilitate learner‑centred instruction aligned with curriculum standards.
source_frameworks: ["UNESCO ICT Competency Framework for Teachers v4 (ICT‑CFT)"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Uses presentation software; uploads resources to Learning Management System (LMS); follows basic digital‑safety rules.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Designs blended lessons; utilises quizzes in LMS; supports students in accessing digital materials.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Applies Universal Design for Learning (UDL); integrates real‑time collaboration tools; differentiates instruction with adaptive software.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Aligns ICT use with pedagogical goals; assesses digital‑literacy growth; mentors peers on technology integration.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads institution‑wide EdTech strategy; evaluates emerging tools; publishes research on ICT‑enhanced learning outcomes.
