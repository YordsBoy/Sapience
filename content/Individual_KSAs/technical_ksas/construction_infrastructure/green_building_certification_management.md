- level: Intermediate
indicator: Conducts energy‑model simulation; completes Indoor Environmental Quality (IEQ) credits; coordinates commissioning.
- level: Advanced
indicator: Manages multi‑disciplinary certification teams; negotiates credits trade‑offs; mentors project teams on integrative design.
- level: Expert
indicator: Advises at portfolio scale; develops zero‑carbon pathways; influences updates to rating‑system standards.
