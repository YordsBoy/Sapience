category: Technical
sector: Education_Training
horizon: perennial
cluster_tags:
  - "Classroom"
  - "Engagement"
description: Establishing positive learning environments through proactive behaviour strategies, culturally responsive practices, and motivational techniques that maximise instructional time and learner engagement.
source_frameworks:
  - "UNESCO ICT Competency Framework for Teachers"
  - "Positive Behavioural Interventions and Supports (PBIS) Framework"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Sets clear expectations; uses seating arrangements; recognises off‑task behaviour triggers.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Applies praise‑to‑correction ratios; implements simple routines; leverages student interests.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Utilises data to adjust management plans; differentiates engagement strategies; applies restorative practices.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Coaches peers on behaviour supports; integrates social‑emotional learning (SEL); analyses engagement analytics.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs school‑wide behaviour frameworks; coaches faculty in multi-tiered support; publishes engagement research; advises education ministries; influences policy.
