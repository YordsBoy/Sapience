    indicator: Differentiates data/analytics layers; lists service operations; retrieves KPIs via OAM; plots cell-level utilization; recognizes anomaly events.
  - level: Basic

  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Implements closed-loop optimization; integrates AI-Ops pipelines; ensures data-governance compliance; clusters slices; automates scaling; reduces drop rate.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads carrier-scale analytics strategies; architects multi-vendor NWDAF; mentors analytics squad; publishes AI-enabled network-ops research; shapes 3GPP analytics standards.
