description: >
  Implements wilderness risk-management plans, conducts safety briefings, and
  responds to incidents following industry guidelines and local regulations.
source_frameworks:
  - "Wilderness Risk Management Conference Guidelines"
  - "Leave No Trace Principles"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies common hazards; carries basic first-aid kit.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Prepares route plans; monitors weather; documents incident reports.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Leads groups; applies risk-assessment matrices; administers wilderness first aid.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Designs safety management plans; trains staff; liaises with EMS/rescue.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Audits multi-site outdoor programs; integrates satellite comms; mentors safety officers.
