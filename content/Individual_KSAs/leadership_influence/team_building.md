---
ksa_id: team_building
label: Team Leadership & Collaboration
category: Leadership
sector: cross_sector
horizon: perennial
cluster_tags: 
  - "Team Leadership"
  - "Collaboration"
description: > 
  Inspires and fosters team commitment, spirit, and trust; facilitates cooperation and motivates team members to accomplish group goals.
source_frameworks:
  - "OPM Leadership Competency – Team Building (inspires team commitment and trust)"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness  
    indicator: Listens to teammates' contributions during group work and meetings; completes assigned team tasks reliably without needing to be prompted to cooperate.
  - level: Basic  
    indicator: Organizes team meetings and encourages participation; addresses minor conflicts with supervision.
  - level: Intermediate  
    indicator: Builds cohesive project teams, defining roles and goals; boosts team morale through recognition and inclusive decision-making.
  - level: Advanced  
    indicator: Leads high-performing teams on complex initiatives; coaches others in collaborative problem-solving and conflict resolution.
  - level: Expert  
    indicator: Cultivates a culture of trust and collaboration organization-wide; designs team-building programs and mentors leaders in team development.
