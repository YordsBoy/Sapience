source_frameworks: ["Raspberry Pi Foundation Digital Making Curriculum","NISTIR 8367 Foundational Cybersecurity Practices for IoT Device Manufacturers"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies breadboard components; uploads sample sketches; measures voltage with multimeter.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Interfaces digital/analog sensors; debounces inputs; sends data via serial or Wi‑Fi.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Utilises interrupt routines; implements MQTT messaging; designs printed‑circuit boards (PCBs) with KiCad.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Optimises power consumption; secures communication with Transport Layer Security (TLS); tests electromagnetic compatibility (EMC).
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads maker‑to‑market IoT productisation; certifies devices for safety; mentors community electronics labs.
