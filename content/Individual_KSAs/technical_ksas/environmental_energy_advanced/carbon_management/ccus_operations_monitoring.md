  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Explains absorption vs. adsorption capture; identifies amine‑scrubbing components; recognises basic monitoring methods, process variables, and alarms.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Tracks capture‑efficiency metrics; collects flue‑gas samples; follows injection‑well safety protocols; logs compression data.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Calibrates gas‑analysis instrumentation; analyses plume‑migration models; adjusts operations; reports to environmental regulators.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Optimises solvent regeneration; integrates carbon‑utilisation reactors; implements real‑time seismic monitoring; Implements digital twins and predictive maintenance.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs multi‑facility CCUS hubs; leads measurement‑verification programs; Establishes MRV protocols for regulatory compliance; influences international CCUS standards.
