ksa_id: audio_post_production_operations
label: Professional Audio Post‑production Operations
category: Technical
sector: Media_Arts_Entertainment
horizon: core
cluster_tags:
  - "Audio"
  - "Post"
description: Editing, mixing, and mastering linear and immersive audio for film, television, and streaming using industry standards—Audio Engineering Society (AES) loudness specs, Dolby Atmos workflows—and ensuring deliverables meet distribution requirements.
source_frameworks:
  - "ITU-R BS.1770-4 Loudness"
  - "Dolby Atmos Home Studio Guidelines"
  - "AES TD1008 Loudness Guidelines"
  - "Dolby Atmos Home Entertainment Mix Spec"
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Identifies digital‑audio‑workstation (DAW) tools; defines LKFS; recognises loudness units full scale (LUFS) meters; imports OMF/AAF sessions.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Cleans clicks; sets rough levels; exports interleaved WAV; performs dialogue editing; applies basic equalisation (EQ) and dynamics; conforms stems to cue sheet.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Uses loudness meter; mixes 5.1 surround; calibrates monitoring environments; meets Integrated Loudness targets per EBU R128; meets −24 LKFS ±2.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Mixes immersive stems; delivers ADM BWF; builds object‑based Atmos mixes; implements noise‑reduction; conducts deliverable QC for streaming platforms.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Supervises multi‑studio post workflows; sets facility standards; mentors re‑recording mixers; chairs immersive-audio panel; publishes audio‑post best practices.
