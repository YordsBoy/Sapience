ksa_id: facility_decommissioning_planning
label: Facility Decommissioning & Abandonment Planning
category: Technical
sector: Mining_Extraction_Oil_Gas
horizon: perennial
cluster_tags: ["Decommissioning","Abandonment","Project Management"]
description: Planning, budgeting, and executing decommissioning of wells, pipelines, and offshore structures in compliance with U.S. Bureau of Safety & Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) and International Association of Oil & Gas Producers (IOGP) guidelines, ensuring safety, environmental protection, and stakeholder engagement.
source_frameworks: ["BSEE Decommissioning Guidance","IOGP Report 646 Decommissioning of Offshore Facilities","ISO 19901‑8 Offshore Decommissioning Planning"]
proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness
    indicator: Lists decommissioning stages; recognises regulatory permits; reviews historical asset data.
  - level: Basic
    indicator: Prepares well‑plugging programmes; estimates abandonment costs; coordinates waste‑management plans.
  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Develops project schedules (PERT/CPM); manages subcontractors; conducts environmental‑impact assessments.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Executes topside and jacket removal; applies reverse‑install techniques; oversees site‑clearance surveys.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads multi‑asset decommissioning portfolios; optimises reuse/recycle options; shapes decommissioning policy frameworks.
