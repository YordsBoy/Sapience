proficiency_levels:
  - level: Awareness

    indicator: Calculates metabolic load; monitors pressure/temperature; supports contingency consumable planning; performs canister swaps, water-quality tests, and leak checks.

    indicator: Analyzes hardware performance; manages microbial control; integrates thermal loops; analyzes cabin-air curves; supports Sabatier reactor operations.

    indicator: Designs closed-loop ECLSS; validates redundancy; conducts fault-tree analyses; troubleshoots subsystem interfaces; recommends mass/energy trade-offs.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Leads ECLSS architecture for multi-year interplanetary missions; chairs standards working groups at NASA/ESA/ISO; publishes peer-reviewed life-support research; mentors next-generation systems engineers.
