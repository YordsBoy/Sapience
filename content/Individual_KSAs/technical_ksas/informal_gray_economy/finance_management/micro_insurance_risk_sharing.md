  - level: Basic

  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Understands exclusions; bundles with micro-credit; tracks renewal.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Participates in group policy; analyses payout triggers; educates community.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Negotiates customised schemes; influences regulators; mentors agent network.
